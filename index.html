Heyooo I'm breakfastb (or chadolbaki) and this is where you can find info about the Christmas cards I'm sending out :3c This is just a very basic site, so if you have questions feel free to contact me!

#info

I do this every year and generally get a decent amount of requests (~50), but due to the cost of stamps/envelopes/etc. I have donations set up at ko-fi! If you can't donate, no worries. I don't give priority to donors (though I really appreciate it!! It helps a lot with this aa) so it's no biggie.

Also because of the high volume and my own schedule with school/work/personal projects, I can't do as many of these as I like and I always end up with some requests unfinished oTL if you absolutely want something, please feel free to check out my commissions! Otherwise, I'll do my absolute best to get as many of these as I can done. Additionally, these will just be traditional sketches. Please keep complex requests to a minimum ; w;)a if I get too many complicated requests it's harder to get through them all.

Lastly, these will get mailed with a small photo print~
